Sandbox Tips
• Sand falls and piles up.
• Water flows around obstacles.
• Fire burns flammable materials.
• Acid eats through most solids.
• Use Ruler for clean lines and Lighter to ignite.
Neon Snake Tips
• Wraps around edges — use that to your advantage.
• Plan ahead: your tail is your biggest enemy.
• Restart anytime to chase a higher score.
Pablo Dash Editor
• Build levels on a horizontal strip.
• Barrier line keeps you grounded; spawn line is the start.
• End portal marks the goal.
• Moving platforms can be horizontal or vertical — set direction in the toolbar.
• Gravity, speed, checkpoints, and color blocks let you shape the feel of the level.
About Pablo's Portal
A compact playground built around three vibes: simulation, arcade, and precision platforming. Everything is tuned to feel like one coherent neon machine.
