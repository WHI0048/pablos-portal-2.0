This build of Pablo's Portal 2.0 is a neon UI shell with a Geometry Dash–style level editor added as "Pablo Dash Editor". The classic physics sandbox, particles, explosions, ruler, lighter, and Neon Snake logic remain removed; their canvases are blank neon stages, ready for future features.
